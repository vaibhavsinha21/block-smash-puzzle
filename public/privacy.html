Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

Block Smash Puzzle ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Locally Stored Data:

  • Game scores, settings, and progress (stored only on your device via localStorage)
  • No personal information (name, email, phone) is ever collected
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties

2. How We Use Data

  • To save your game progress, high scores, and achievements locally on your device
  • To track your level, streaks, and daily challenges locally

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not currently use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All game data stays on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is fully compliant with COPPA.

5. Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app. We do not store personal data on our servers.

6. Your Rights

  • You can clear all local data by clearing app storage
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all data

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect data. All network communications use HTTPS encryption.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@blocksmashpuzzle.com
