## Description
Place blocks. Clear lines. Score big!

Block Smash Puzzle is a beautifully designed block puzzle game that's easy to pick up but impossible to put down.

HOW TO PLAY:
- Drag and drop blocks from the tray onto the 8×8 grid
- Complete a full row or column to clear it and earn points
- Plan your moves carefully — placed blocks stay on the board!
- Use all 3 blocks to receive a new set
- The game ends when no more blocks can fit

WHAT MAKES IT SPECIAL:
- 28 unique block shapes keep every game fresh
- Chain line clears for massive combo multipliers
- Satisfying particle effects and sound design
- Auto-saves your progress automatically
- Works completely offline
- No time limits — play at your own pace
- Clean, modern dark UI designed for focus

PERFECT FOR:
- Short commutes and waiting rooms
- Brain training and spatial reasoning
- Unwinding after a long day
- Competing with friends via score sharing
